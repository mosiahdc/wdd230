About Negros

Don't miss it in Negros

Demographics

Negros is the fourth largest and third most populous island in the Philippines, with a total land area of 13,309 km. The coastal zone of the southern part of Negros is identified as a site of highest marine biodiversity importance in the Coral Triangle.

Events

  • 1. Panaad sa Negros Festival - Panaad is the Hiligaynon word for "vow" or "promise" coming from the root-word saad; the festival is a form of thanksgiving to Divine Providence and commemoration of a vow in exchange for a good life.

  • 2. Masskara Festival - Prominent feature of the festival and is always adorned with smiling faces, giving rise to Bacolod being called the "City of Smiles".

  • 3. Dinagsa Festival - Features Ati Tribes for the main event, the Street, and Arena Dance Competition. People attending the street dance competition uses a white shirt to show off various colors of paint they got by attending the festivity.
