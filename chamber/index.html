Events

  • 1. Panaad sa Negros Festival - Panaad is the Hiligaynon word for "vow" or "promise" coming from the root-word saad; the festival is a form of thanksgiving to Divine Providence and commemoration of a vow in exchange for a good life.
  • 2. Masskara Festival - Prominent feature of the festival and is always adorned with smiling faces, giving rise to Bacolod being called the "City of Smiles".
